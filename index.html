Virtual Astronomy Software Talks

A monthly seminar on astronomy software

Astronomy research is supported by a large number of software projects, often working behind the scenes. The VAST seminar series puts this software in the spotlight, allowing developers to share their libraries and projects with the community. Taking place virtually, VAST seminars can reach the global astronomy community, and will feature two projects and an open discussion each month, allowing for meaningful communication with the community.

Organizers

  • Alice Allen (ASCL)
  • Amruta Jaodand (Caltech)
  • Catherine Slaughter (Leiden University)
  • Michael Zingale (Stony Brook)

Logistics

When

Talks will be monthly on the third Wednesday of each month at 11 am East coast time (US) via Zoom.

Format

Each VAST will consist of two 20 minute talks followed by an open discussion.

Join

Join the VAST mailing list to receive the Zoom links: VAST google group.

Propose a talk

The organizing committee will select the talks for each monthly seminar. If you wish to propose a talk, fill an issue on the VAST Github page.
